Common questions

How much does a stairlift or bathtub conversion cost?

Every staircase and bathroom is different, so we quote after the free in-home assessment. You get a clear written quote on the spot, with no cost and no obligation.

How long does installation take?

Most straight stairlifts are installed in a few hours, often within days of ordering. Curved lifts take a little longer because the rail is custom made for your staircase. A bathtub conversion is typically done in about 2 hours.

Can I still have a bath after a bathtub conversion?

Yes. The Quick Tub cap drops into the walk-thru opening and seals watertight, so your tub holds a full bath again. It goes in and out in seconds with no tools.

Who installs and services the equipment?

Our own local team. The people who measure your home are the ones who install the equipment and look after it afterward, backed by full manufacturer warranties.

Is the in-home assessment really free?

Yes. We visit your home, photo-measure your stairs or bathroom, show you the options, and leave you a written quote. There is no charge and no obligation to buy.

What happens if we move or no longer need the lift?

Stairlifts can be removed and the staircase restored. Ask us about your options during the assessment and we will walk you through them.

Is a stairlift the same as a chair lift or stair lift?

Yes. Stairlift, stair lift and chair lift for stairs are different names for the same thing: a motorized seat that carries you up and down the staircase on a rail. We sell, install and service both straight and curved models.

How is Quick Tub different from a walk-in tub?

A walk-in tub replaces your bathtub and usually means a renovation. Quick Tub converts the tub you already own into a step-in shower in about 2 hours, and the drop-in cap still gives you a full, deep bath whenever you want one.
